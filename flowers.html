Flowers and Their Details

Rose

Scientific Name: Rosa

Color: Red, Pink, White, Yellow

Bloom Season: Spring to Fall

Uses: Bouquets, perfumes, symbolic gifts

Sunflower

Scientific Name: Helianthus annuus

Color: Yellow

Bloom Season: Summer

Uses: Seeds, oil, ornamental

Lily

Scientific Name: Lilium

Color: White, Pink, Orange

Bloom Season: Summer

Uses: Perfume, decoration, ceremonies

Marigold

Scientific Name: Tagetes

Color: Orange, Yellow

Bloom Season: Summer to Fall

Uses: Decoration, cultural uses, pest control
