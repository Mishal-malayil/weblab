MEA ENGINEERING COLLEGE PERINTHALMANNA

MEA Engineering College, Perinthalmanna, established in 2002, is a self-financing Muslim minority institution affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE. Located at Vengoor, about 12 km from Perinthalmanna town, the college has a scenic 28-acre campus with modern facilities like hostels, labs, library, and transport. It offers B.Tech programs in Civil, Mechanical, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics & Communication, and IT, along with M.Tech courses in select specializations. Known as the first NAAC-accredited self-financing engineering college in Malappuram district, MEA provides quality technical education with opportunities for campus placements in reputed companies.
